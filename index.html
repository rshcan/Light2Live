Imperial Grace

A downloadable game for Windows, macOS, and Linux

Imperial Grace, is a visual novel / otome game, set in a medieval world with facultative managment features, focusing on politics and romance.


Play as the new Empress of Calydia, an ambitious and cunning woman. Thanks to the many choices offered throughout the game and the avatar customisation options, become the Empress you want to be!


Guide your subjects by addressing their petitions, and navigate the crises unsettling your realm. It's up to you to tackle the many dilemmas that will change the history of Calidya... 

Enjoy the story with infinite resources, or spice up your game by wisely managing your kingdom's resources and politics, making each choice even more difficult.

Don‚Äôt like management features? Enjoy our game's ‚Äústory mode‚Äù! But be careful... even if your resources are infinite, your subjects' patience is not. You will still need to make careful choices...


Fall under the charm of the four* love interests and their flamboyant personalities, and discover the kingdom of Calidya through colourful and well-developed secondary characters.


*The Master of Murmurs will be a DLC romance.

  • More than 150 000 words of court intrigue and romance
  • A mature story with (optional) consensual spicy moments (click here to see the full list of our trigger warnings.)
  • Hundreds of meaningful choices to shape your personality and your kingdom futur.
  • 20 unique atmospheric music tracks 
  • 15 beautiful illustrations (CG) reflecting your main character customisation


The game will be released in Spring 2024, to follow the development, learn fun details and receive extra rewards you can join our newsletter! <3

- There is rarely direct influence between what you do in diplomacy and the main story (no you did X so Y happens in the main story), but at some key moment if a country like you they will help, if they dislike you they will make it harder for you and some decision might have further consequence on the long term (like epilogue wise).
